
## Release 0

- tampilkan status title, point, level, player name, age, dan gender, urutkan berdasarkan level dari yang terbesar ke yang terkecil

SELECT title, point, level, name, age FROM Players JOIN Status
ON Status.playerId = Players.id
ORDER BY level DESC;

## Release 1

- tampilkan `player` yang memiliki `jumlah kartu`, dan berjenis kelamin `male` serta tampilkan 3 teratas dari yang paling banyak jumlah kartunya.

SELECT COUNT(*) AS totalcard, Players.id, Players.name, age, gender FROM Players JOIN Cards
ON Players.id = Cards.playerId
WHERE Players.gender = 'Male'
GROUP BY Players.name
ORDER BY totalcard DESC, Players.id
LIMIT 3;
